\begin{abstract}
\small
This paper presents a multi-agent workflow system for Bitcoin investment analysis. The system integrates ensemble machine learning (Linear Regression 82\%, ARIMA, Seasonal) with five specialized AI analysts, anonymous peer review, and adversarial validation. Experimental results yield a \textbf{BUY} recommendation with \textbf{45\%} position size, validated by Red-Blue team scoring \textbf{7.1/10}.
\end{abstract}

\vspace{-0.2cm}
\subsection{Phase 1: Ensemble Prediction}
Three models generate predictions with weighted fusion:
\begin{equation}
\hat{y} = 0.50 \cdot \hat{y}_{LR} + 0.30 \cdot \hat{y}_{ARIMA} + 0.20 \cdot \hat{y}_{Seasonal}
\end{equation}

\subsection{Phase 2: Multi-Agent Analysis}
Five specialized agents (Technical, Industry, Financial, Market, Risk) independently analyze data. Bayesian signal fusion combines their outputs:
\begin{equation}
P(Bull|S) = \frac{\prod_i P(S_i|Bull) \cdot P(Bull)}{\sum_H \prod_i P(S_i|H) \cdot P(H)}
\end{equation}

\subsection{Phase 3: Anonymous Peer Review}
Analyses are anonymized (A-E) to eliminate authority bias. Quality score:
$Q_i = \sum_d \omega_d \cdot \bar{s}_i^d$ where $\bar{s}_i^d$ is the average peer score.

\subsection{Phase 4: Red-Blue Validation}
Red Team attacks the decision; Blue Team defends. Judge synthesizes final score.

\section{Conclusion}
The multi-agent workflow successfully produced a validated BUY decision with 45\% position sizing. Key innovations include: (1) anonymous peer review eliminating authority bias, (2) Red-Blue adversarial validation, and (3) automated risk budget calculation ensuring disciplined position sizing.
